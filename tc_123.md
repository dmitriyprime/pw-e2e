# Manual Test Cases — Store Switcher Clean URL (tc_123)

**Context:** The store switcher now redirects to the target store's homepage instead of preserving the current page URL. These cases verify that switcher-dependent functionality is not broken by this change.  
**Environment:** `https://ecommerce-dev.gomage.dev`

---

## Pre-conditions

- Clean-URL switcher is deployed on DEV.
- At least two store views are active with different currencies and at least one store-scoped catalog price rule configured.
- A test customer account exists (email/password known).

---

## TC-123-01 — Currency Mapping

**Goal:** Verify the correct currency is applied in all areas after switching stores via the new clean-URL switcher.

**Steps:**

1. Open any product listing page (PLP) on Store A (e.g. `/en/`).
2. Note the currency symbol and a product price.
3. Use the store switcher to switch to Store B (e.g. `/cn/`). Confirm the browser lands on Store B's homepage.
4. Check the currency symbol in the header/price display.
5. Open a product detail page (PDP) and check the price.
6. Add the product to cart; open the cart and check the price and currency symbol.

**Expected result:**

- All prices on homepage, PLP, PDP, and cart display in Store B's configured currency (symbol and value).
- No prices are shown in Store A's currency after switching.
- The currency cookie (`store_currency` or equivalent) matches Store B's currency code.

**Pass criteria:** Currency symbol and converted price are consistent with Store B's currency across PLP, PDP, and cart.

---

## TC-123-02 — Catalog Price Rules

**Goal:** Verify that a website-level catalog price rule is correctly applied on both store views after switching via the clean-URL switcher.

**Pre-condition:** A catalog price rule (e.g. 10% discount on a specific product or category) is active for the shared website. Both Store A and Store B belong to this website, so the rule applies to both.

**Steps:**

1. Open a product PDP on Store A that is covered by the active price rule.
2. Confirm the discounted price is shown (original price struck through, sale price visible).
3. Note the displayed sale price.
4. Use the store switcher to switch to Store B. Confirm redirect lands on Store B's homepage.
5. Navigate to the same product PDP on Store B.
6. Confirm the discounted price is shown and the discount amount is consistent with Store A (accounting for any currency conversion).
7. Open the cart on Store B; add the product if not already added and verify the rule-adjusted price appears in cart totals.

**Expected result:**

- The price rule is applied consistently on both store views — no store loses the discount after switching.
- Cart totals reflect the discounted price on Store B.
- No JavaScript or network errors related to price calculation in the browser console.

**Pass criteria:** Discounted price renders correctly on both store views before and after switching; cart totals match.

---

## TC-123-03 — Customer Session and Cart After Store Switch

**Goal:** Verify that a logged-in customer's session and cart contents are preserved after switching stores with the new clean-URL switcher.

**Steps:**

1. Log in as a test customer on Store A.
2. Add 2 products to the cart; confirm cart item count (e.g. "2 items" in header).
3. Use the store switcher to switch to Store B. Confirm redirect lands on Store B's homepage.
4. Check that the customer is still logged in (name visible in header, "Sign In" link is absent).
5. Open the cart on Store B and verify item count and products are intact (or empty if stores have separate carts — note which behavior is observed).
6. Switch back to Store A; confirm session and cart state are unchanged.

**Expected result:**

- Customer remains logged in after switching — no unexpected logout.
- Cart is not silently emptied by the switch.
- No "session expired" or 401/403 errors in the browser console.

**Pass criteria:** Login state is maintained across the store switch; no session loss or console auth errors.
